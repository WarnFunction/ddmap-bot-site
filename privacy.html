Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

The Deep Desert Companion Bot respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the bot collects, how it's used, and your rights.

Information Collected

  • Interaction Data: Commands you invoke (/ddmap, /ddreset) and timestamps.
  • Caching Metadata: Screenshot timestamps stored locally to manage cache invalidation.

Usage of Information

  • To generate and serve up-to-date map and storm information upon user request.
  • To manage caching logic, ensuring efficient responses.

Data Storage & Retention

All data is stored locally on your bot host. No external servers or databases are used. Cached images expire automatically after the next Coriolis storm reset.

No Personal Data

The bot does not collect or store personal data, user IDs, or message contents beyond slash-command interactions.

Third-Party Services

No third-party analytics or tracking services are employed. Puppeteer is used solely for map scraping; no logs are shared.
